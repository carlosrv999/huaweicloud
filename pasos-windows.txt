1. curl -LO "https://dl.k8s.io/release/v1.21.0/bin/windows/amd64/kubectl.exe"

2. Crear la carpeta kube en C:\, y poner ahi el kubectl.exe

3. Agregar C:\kube al PATH

4. mkdir -p $HOME/.kube

5. mv -f kubeconfig.json $HOME/.kube/config
